Our Mission

About Fortis

We built Fortis because musicians deserve better — better protection, better pay, and a real community.

🛡️

The Problem

Musicians lose billions every year to unfair platform cuts, AI theft of their work, non-payment by venues, and opaque streaming royalties. The system is broken.

⚡

Our Solution

Fortis is a platform where artists own their rights, fans pay fairly, and every transaction is transparent. AI-powered Protection detects unauthorized use. A simple 10% all-inclusive fee replaces hidden charges.

🌍

Who We Are

Founded by Zoran, a music-industry veteran who got tired of watching talented artists get taken advantage of. Built in public, with 97 early members already on board. Servers in Frankfurt, EU — privacy-first.
